Help & Guidelines

  • Login using your staff username/email and password.
  • To create a reservation, select a room type and enter valid check-in/check-out dates.
  • Check-out date must be after check-in date.
  • Use “View Reservation” to search by reservation number (e.g., RES-000001).
  • Use “Billing” to calculate total (nights × rate per night) and print the bill.
  • Always verify guest details and contact number before saving.
Tip: If you get an error, read the message shown on screen. Most errors are due to invalid dates or an incorrect reservation number.
